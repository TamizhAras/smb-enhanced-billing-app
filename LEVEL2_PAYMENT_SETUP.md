## 🎯 **How It Works:**

### **For Business Owners (Like Sarah):**
1. **Create Invoice** - Same as before
2. **Click "Pay Now"** - New button next to "Record Payment"
3. **Show Customer** - Beautiful payment options modal opens
4. **Customer Pays** - Using their preferred method
5. **Mark as Paid** - When payment is received

### **For Customers:**
1. **Receive Invoice** - Via email/WhatsApp as before
2. **Click Payment Options** - See all available payment methods
3. **Choose Method:**
   - 📱 **UPI** - Click to open UPI app
   - 📷 **QR Code** - Scan with any payment app
   - 💳 **Razorpay** - Pay with cards/UPI/net banking
   - 🌍 **PayPal** - International payments
   - 🏦 **Bank Transfer** - Copy bank details

## 📋 **Setup Instructions:**

### **Step 1: Configure Your Payment Details**
1. Go to **Enhanced Billing** → **Settings Tab**
2. Scroll to **"Payment Gateway Configuration"**
3. Fill in your business details:
   ```
   Business Name: Your Business Name
   UPI ID: yourbusiness@paytm
   Bank Account: Your account number
   IFSC Code: Your bank's IFSC
   ```

### **Step 2: Enable Payment Methods**
- ✅ **UPI Payments** - Always enabled (free)
- ✅ **Bank Transfer** - Always enabled (free)
- ⚙️ **Razorpay** - Requires API keys (optional)
- ⚙️ **PayPal** - For international customers (optional)

### **Step 3: Test the Payment Flow**
1. Create a test invoice
2. Click **"💳 Pay Now"** button
3. See the payment options modal
4. Test QR code generation
5. Test payment links
